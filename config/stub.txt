gameloop
read remaining:string(15)
read current:string(1)
read h:int w:int
loop h read line:string(w)
write (0,0) (0,1) (1,0) (2,0)

STATEMENT
Place all polyominoes to form predefined 2D shape

INPUT
remaining: IDs of remaining polyominoes
current: ID of current polyomino to place on board
line: single line of board - place your polyominoes only on 'O' symbols
